Farm Dinners
May 2026 · Our first evenings at the farm

We can't wait to see you up on the farm. We're a family farm on the North Fork of the Skagit River outside La Conner, and these dinners are our favorite nights of the year: everything Skagit Valley and Swan House have to offer, cooked by the San Fermo team and served in the garden pergola as the evening comes on.

Driving directions
Weekend traffic from Seattle can be heavy, so give yourself a little extra time. Coming from the south, take I-5 to Exit 221 (Conway / Lake McMurray) and head west toward La Conner. Just past the North Fork bridge, turn left onto Dodge Valley Rd. In about 0.6 miles, turn left onto Landing Rd — it's a bit of a blind turn, so take it slow. Follow the bends and continue past the private road sign.

From the bar
Good to know
Dress for the farm
Dinner is under a covered garden pergola, open to the evening air. Comfortable walking shoes, a warm layer for after sunset, and sun protection on bright days.
The terrain
Farm tours follow walking paths over uneven ground — another vote for sturdy shoes.
Seating
All 26 guests share three communal tables. Joining a larger group? Reach out and we'll seat you together.
Wine & zero-proof
Paired wine and bubbles are included, and we pour excellent non-alcoholic wines for anyone who prefers them.
Out by the river
We sit right along the Skagit River, so bees, wasps, and mosquitos come with the setting. We suggest bringing a little bug spray — and if anyone has a serious sting allergy, please weigh that carefully.
The animals
Tours offer a chance to visit the livestock. Say hello — gently, and at your own discretion.
Restrooms
The farm septic system is modest, so we provide comfortable temporary restrooms for the evening.
Dogs
We love them, but the livestock don't — please leave pups at home.
Photos
Please take pictures and share the evening. We appreciate the support!
Tickets
Bookings are final, but tickets are transferable — if plans change, contact us and we'll help hand them to new guests. Severe weather is the one exception: we'll cancel and make it right.
